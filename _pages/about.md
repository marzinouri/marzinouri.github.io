I'm Marzia Nouri, a Master’s student in Computer Science at [McGill University](https://www.mcgill.ca/) and [Mila](https://mila.quebec/en), supervised by [Prof. Siva Reddy](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=LZZAAtoAAAAJ&hl=en), where I work on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs). My research focuses on interpretability, evaluation, and responsible AI for language models.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from [Isfahan University of Technology](https://english.iut.ac.ir/). Previously, I worked as an R&D Data Scientist at [Tapsell](https://tapsell.com/en/), developing AI-driven systems such as conversational AI, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, and multi-agent frameworks. I have also contributed as a research assistant to research groups at [Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI)](https://www.hbku.edu.qa/en/qcri), [HyperMatrix Lab](https://hypermatrix.cs.dal.ca/) at [Dalhousie University](https://www.dal.ca/), and Sharif University of Technology.

My research contributions have appeared at venues including [EACL](https://2026.eacl.org/), [EMNLP](https://2024.emnlp.org/), [COLM](https://colmweb.org/), and [AACL](https://aclanthology.org/events/aacl-2023).

For more details, you can view my [CV](https://marzinouri.github.io/assets/pdf/MarziaNouri-CV.pdf) and explore my [Publications](https://marzinouri.github.io/publications/).
